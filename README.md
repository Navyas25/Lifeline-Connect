# Lifeline-Connect
**🩸 LifeLine Connect – Emergency Blood Donation Platform**

LifeLine Connect is a web-based platform designed to connect **blood donors, hospitals, and patients** during medical emergencies. In many regions, patients and hospitals face serious difficulties in finding the required blood group quickly due to the absence of a centralized and efficient system. This delay can lead to critical situations where patients do not receive blood in time. LifeLine Connect aims to solve this problem by creating a digital platform that makes it easier to locate available blood donors and manage emergency blood requests.

The platform allows hospitals or individuals to post **urgent blood requests**, which can then be viewed on a dashboard. Registered donors can list their **blood group, location, and contact details**, making it easier for hospitals or patients to identify and contact suitable donors quickly. The system also includes a dashboard that displays the **total number of donors, hospitals, and emergency requests**, helping administrators track and manage blood availability more efficiently.

This project contributes to the **United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 3: Good Health and Well-Being**, by improving access to lifesaving blood during emergencies and supporting better healthcare outcomes. It also supports **SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure**, by using digital technology to improve healthcare infrastructure and communication.

Overall, LifeLine Connect helps **bridge the gap between blood donors and patients**, enabling faster response during emergencies and potentially saving lives.
